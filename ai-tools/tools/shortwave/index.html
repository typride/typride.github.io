Shortwave

Productivity & Knowledge Management active ★ 4.2 freemium · $12/month Free tier available

Shortwave is an AI-powered email client designed to transform inbox management. It leverages AI to provide smart summaries of email threads, draft replies in a personalized tone, and offer custom AI filters for automated inbox organization. It aims to make email a more efficient and less overwhelming communication channel.

Try Shortwave →

Key Features

AI Email SummariesAutomatically condenses long email threads into concise summaries, allowing users to quickly understand the gist of conversations without reading every message.
AI Reply DraftingGenerates context-aware email replies in a personalized tone, significantly speeding up response times and reducing writing effort.
Custom AI FiltersEnables users to create personalized AI-powered rules to automatically label, star, archive, or perform other actions on incoming emails, enhancing inbox organization.
Inbox AutomationBeyond filters, it allows for complex workflow automation directly from the inbox, connecting email actions to other business tools.

Use Cases

  • Efficiently summarizing long email threads to quickly grasp key information
  • Drafting quick and contextually relevant email replies with AI assistance
  • Automating inbox organization with custom AI filters for labeling, starring, and archiving
  • Managing and prioritizing email communications for enhanced productivity

Pros

  • Advanced AI for smart summaries and personalized reply drafting
  • Customizable AI filters for powerful inbox automation and organization
  • Designed to significantly reduce email overload and improve focus
  • Integrates with various business tools for streamlined workflows

Cons

  • AI-generated drafts can sometimes sound generic, requiring human refinement
  • Primarily optimized for Gmail users, potentially limiting appeal for others
  • Higher-tier pricing can be a significant investment for advanced features
