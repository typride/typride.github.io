Resemble AI

Audio & Music Generation active ★ 4.5 freemium · $0/month Free tier available

Resemble AI is a leading AI voice cloning and speech synthesis platform that enables enterprises to create ultra-realistic AI voices. It also offers advanced deepfake detection capabilities across audio, video, and images, ensuring content authenticity and security. The platform is known for its open-source Chatterbox model and PerTh watermarking for provenance tracking.

Key Features

Chatterbox Generative Voice AIUltra-realistic text-to-speech with zero-shot voice cloning from 5 seconds of audio, supporting 23 languages. It's open-source, production-ready, and includes PerTh watermarking.
DETECT-3B Omni Multimodal Deepfake DetectionState-of-the-art deepfake detection across audio, video, and images, battle-tested against 160+ generative AI models with 99.8% accuracy. Offers real-time analysis and explainable AI.
PerTh WatermarkPerceptually transparent watermarking that embeds invisible, robust markers into AI-generated content for provenance tracking, surviving compression and editing.
On-Premise DeploymentAllows enterprises to deploy Chatterbox and DETECT-3B Omni within their own infrastructure, ensuring zero data egress and full control over models for strict security and compliance requirements.
Emotion & Expression ControlProvides unique exaggeration parameters and paralinguistic tags (e.g., [laugh], [sigh]) for highly natural and expressive speech generation.
Speaker VerificationBiometric voice verification that authenticates speakers in real-time, protecting against voice identity fraud and unauthorized access.

Use Cases

  • Creating ultra-realistic AI voices for various applications like narration, virtual assistants, and entertainment
  • Detecting deepfakes in audio, video, and images for security, fraud prevention, and content authenticity
  • Voice cloning from minimal audio samples (e.g., 5 seconds) for personalized content
  • On-premise deployment of generative voice and deepfake detection models for enhanced security and data control
  • Audio enhancement and speaker verification for improved audio quality and security

Pros

  • Ultra-realistic voice cloning and speech synthesis with emotion and expression control
  • Comprehensive multimodal deepfake detection (audio, video, image) with high accuracy (99.8%)
  • Open-source Chatterbox model available for self-hosting and full ownership
  • PerTh watermarking for imperceptible and robust content provenance tracking
  • Flexible deployment options including cloud, on-premise, and containerized environments
  • Zero-shot voice cloning from very short audio samples

Cons

  • Per-second billing for various services can be complex to manage for some users
  • Advanced enterprise features and on-premise solutions may have a higher barrier to entry for smaller teams or individuals
  • The quality of cloned voices can still vary depending on the input audio quality and length
