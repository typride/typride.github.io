Luma AI

Video Generation active ★ 4.5 freemium · $29/month Free tier available

Luma AI is a groundbreaking platform that excels in AI-powered 3D capture and video generation, notably through its Dream Machine. It allows users to transform 2D images and videos into realistic, explorable 3D models and generate high-quality, cinematic video content from text prompts. Its unique selling proposition lies in its ability to bridge the gap between real-world capture and generative AI, offering unparalleled realism and creative control for creators and developers.
