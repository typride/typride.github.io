Together AI

LLM Platforms & APIs active ★ 4.5 paid · $0.001/1k tokens Free tier available

Together AI provides a robust cloud platform for developers to deploy, fine-tune, and serve open-source large language models efficiently. It stands out by offering competitive pricing and high-performance inference, making advanced LLM capabilities accessible without the overhead of managing complex infrastructure. Its focus on open-source models empowers users with flexibility and transparency, fostering innovation in AI development.
