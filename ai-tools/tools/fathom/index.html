Fathom

Productivity & Knowledge Management active ★ 4.5 freemium · $19/month Free tier available

Fathom is a free AI meeting assistant that records, transcribes, and summarizes virtual meetings on Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. It provides instant AI call summaries, action items, and highlights, allowing users to focus on the conversation rather than note-taking. Its ability to generate shockingly accurate transcripts and deliver consistent quality across calls makes it a valuable tool for enhancing meeting productivity.

AI Meeting SummariesGenerates instant, concise AI summaries of meetings, highlighting key decisions and action items.

Highlights and ClipsEnables users to create and share important moments from meetings as clips or highlights.

Use Cases

  • Automating meeting note-taking and transcription for virtual calls
  • Generating instant summaries and action items from meetings
  • Creating shareable clips and highlights from recorded sessions
  • Improving focus during meetings by eliminating manual note-taking

Pros

  • Offers a robust free tier with unlimited recordings and transcriptions
  • Provides highly accurate transcripts and concise AI summaries
  • Seamless integration with popular video conferencing platforms (Zoom, Meet, Teams)
  • Features like clips and playlists enhance post-meeting review

Cons

  • Advanced AI features and expanded integrations are primarily available in paid plans
  • Reliance on internet connection for real-time transcription and AI processing
  • Some users might find the free version's features limited for extensive team collaboration
