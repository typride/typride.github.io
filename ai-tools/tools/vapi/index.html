Vapi

Agent Frameworks active ★ 3.5 freemium · $0.05/min Free tier available Verified Feb 2025

Vapi is a developer platform designed for building, testing, and deploying advanced AI voice agents. It offers a highly configurable API-first approach, enabling developers to create human-like conversational experiences with low latency. The platform supports both inbound and outbound calls, making it suitable for scaling phone operations and integrating with existing workflows.

Key Features

API-Native PlatformEverything is exposed as an API, providing developers with extensive control and integration capabilities for building voice agents.
Bring Your Own ModelsAllows users to integrate their preferred transcription, LLM, and text-to-speech models, offering flexibility and leveraging existing investments.
Tool CallingEnables voice agents to intelligently fetch data and perform actions by plugging into external APIs and services.
Multilingual SupportSupports over 100 languages, allowing for global deployment of voice agents and catering to diverse user bases.
Real-time PerformanceOffers sub-500ms latency and enterprise-grade reliability for natural, human-like conversational experiences.
Automated Testing & A/B ExperimentsProvides tools for designing test suites and running A/B experiments to optimize agent performance and identify potential issues.

Use Cases

  • Building AI voice assistants for inbound customer service
  • Automating outbound sales or support calls
  • Integrating conversational AI into web and mobile applications

Pros

  • Highly configurable and API-native, offering extensive customization for developers
  • Supports bringing your own LLM, TTS, and STT models for flexibility and cost control
  • Features like tool calling, automated testing, and A/B experiments enhance agent capabilities and optimization
  • Designed for enterprise-grade reliability, scalability, and security with sub-500ms latency

Cons

  • Pricing can be complex due to usage-based model and separate charges for underlying AI models
  • Requires technical expertise for full utilization of its developer-centric features
  • Some user reviews indicate mixed experiences, suggesting potential areas for improvement in user support or ease of use
