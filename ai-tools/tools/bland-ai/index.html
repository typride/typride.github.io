Bland AI

Sales & GTM active ★ 4.2 freemium · $0.14/min (usage-based) Free tier available

Bland AI is an enterprise-grade platform designed to automate inbound and outbound phone calls using highly realistic, human-sounding AI agents. It offers robust infrastructure and APIs for building, testing, and scaling AI-powered phone services, integrating seamlessly into existing tech stacks for sales and customer support. The platform focuses on low-latency, real-time performance and provides deep control over call routing and workflows.
