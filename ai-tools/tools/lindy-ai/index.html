Lindy AI

Automation & Agents active ★ 4.5 freemium · $49/month Free tier available

Lindy AI is a no-code platform for building and deploying AI agents to automate business workflows. It allows users to create custom agents that can interact with various applications and services, such as email, calendars, and CRMs, to perform tasks and streamline processes without writing any code.
