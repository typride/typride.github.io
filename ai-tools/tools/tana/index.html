Tana

Productivity & Knowledge Management active ★ 4.5 freemium · $10/month Free tier available

Tana is an AI-powered knowledge management and outlining tool that helps users organize information, connect ideas, and generate content. It offers a unique approach to note-taking by combining structured data with free-form text, allowing for powerful querying and relationship mapping between different pieces of information.

Key Features

SupertaggingAllows users to define custom schemas and properties for notes, turning them into structured data for powerful querying and organization.
AI CommandsProvides AI-powered features for summarizing, generating, and extracting information directly within notes, enhancing productivity.
Live Search and QueriesEnables dynamic searching and filtering of notes based on their content and structured properties, creating powerful dashboards and views.
Bi-directional LinkingConnects notes and ideas seamlessly, fostering a networked thought process and easy navigation between related concepts.
