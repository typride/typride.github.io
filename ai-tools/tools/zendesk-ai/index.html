Zendesk AI

Sales & GTM active ★ 4.0 paid · $50/user/month (as add-on) Verified Feb 2025

Zendesk AI revolutionizes customer support operations by integrating intelligent bots, enhancing self-service options, and boosting agent productivity. It leverages generative AI to quickly route tickets, analyze customer sentiment, and identify intent, leading to faster resolution times. This suite of AI features is designed to deliver exceptional customer service at scale.

Intelligent BotsAutomate responses and handle routine queries, improving efficiency and reducing agent workload.
Agent AssistProvides agents with real-time suggestions for responses and actions, speeding up resolution and ensuring consistency.
Ticket Triage and RoutingAutomatically routes tickets to the most appropriate agent or department, saving time and improving first-contact resolution.
