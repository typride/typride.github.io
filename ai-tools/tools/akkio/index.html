Akkio

Data Analysis & BI active ★ 4.2 enterprise

Akkio is a no-code AI platform that empowers businesses, particularly media agencies, to leverage advanced forecasting and data analysis. It simplifies the creation and deployment of AI models, enabling users to automate campaign workflows, gain predictive insights, and make informed decisions without extensive data science expertise. Akkio focuses on transforming fragmented data into actionable intelligence across the entire campaign lifecycle.

Chat with DataEnables users to interact with their datasets using natural language queries to uncover insights, generate reports, and perform ad-hoc analysis.
No-Code AI ModelingAllows for the rapid building and deployment of machine learning models for predictive analytics, forecasting, and classification without requiring any coding knowledge.
Generative BI DashboardsAutomatically creates insightful business intelligence dashboards and reports, transforming raw data into visual and actionable summaries.
