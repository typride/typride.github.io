Wispr Flow

Productivity & Knowledge Management active ★ 4.5 freemium · $15/month Free tier available

Wispr Flow is an AI-powered voice dictation tool that transcribes speech into text across any application on desktop and mobile. It stands out by automatically removing filler words, correcting transcription errors in real-time, and allowing users to create custom voice commands (snippets) for inserting boilerplate text. It's designed for users who want to write faster and more naturally by speaking.

Key Features

AI Auto-EditsCleans up your speech in real-time by removing filler words, stutters, and disfluencies, resulting in clean, readable text.
Custom DictionaryAllows you to teach the AI custom words, names, and jargon to ensure they are transcribed correctly every time.
Voice SnippetsCreate custom voice commands to insert frequently used text, such as email templates, addresses, or code blocks.
Command ModeUse voice commands to edit and format text, such as 'delete last sentence' or 'add a new paragraph'.

Use Cases

  • Drafting emails, documents, and messages by voice
  • Transcribing personal notes and thoughts hands-free
  • Improving writing speed for individuals with dyslexia or other writing challenges

Pros

  • Extremely accurate transcription that learns your vocabulary
  • AI auto-edits remove filler words like 'um' and 'uh' automatically
  • Works system-wide in any application on Mac, Windows, iOS, and Android
  • Customizable voice snippets save time on repetitive typing

Cons

  • The free tier has a weekly word limit
  • Requires a constant internet connection to function
