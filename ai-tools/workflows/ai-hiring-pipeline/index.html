AI-Powered Hiring Pipeline

An automated workflow for sourcing, screening, and interviewing candidates using AI tools. Reduces time-to-hire by over 50% by automating candidate sourcing, initial screening, asynchronous video interviews, and response analysis -- letting recruiters focus on high-value interactions with top candidates.

recruitersfoundershr-teams
🌐 View in interactive graph →

Workflow

Step 1
Candidate sourcing & enrichment
Build a list of potential candidates based on job criteria (skills, experience, location). Enrich the list with contact information and professional background data.
Apollo.io
Input: Job requisition with required skills, experience, and location
Output: Enriched candidate list with contact details and background
Enriched candidate list with contact details and background
Step 2
Initial screening & outreach
Deploy an AI chatbot on the careers page to answer candidate questions and perform initial screening. Send automated outreach emails to sourced candidates.
Intercom Fin
Input: Candidate list and screening criteria
Output: Qualified candidate shortlist from chatbot and outreach responses
Qualified candidate shortlist from chatbot and outreach responses
Step 3
Asynchronous video interviews
Invite qualified candidates to a one-way video interview with a pre-recorded AI avatar asking a standard set of screening questions.
HeyGen
Input: Shortlisted candidates and interview question set
Output: Recorded video interview responses
Recorded video interview responses
Step 4
Interview analysis & scoring
Transcribe video interview responses. Analyze transcripts against a predefined rubric, score answers, and summarize candidate strengths and weaknesses.
Claude
Alternatives: ChatGPT
Input: Video interview transcripts and scoring rubric
Output: Scored candidate summaries with strengths/weaknesses
Scored candidate summaries with strengths/weaknesses
Step 5
Shortlist & human review
The hiring manager reviews AI-generated shortlist, scores, and summaries to select final candidates for live interviews.
Input: AI-scored candidate summaries
Output: Final candidate shortlist for live interviews
