AI Video Production Pipeline

A fully-automated pipeline for creating short-form videos, from script to final edit. This workflow is ideal for producing content for platforms like TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels, leveraging AI for every step of the creative process.

marketerscreators
🌐 View in interactive graph →

Workflow

Step 1
Script Generation
Generate a concise, engaging video script based on a topic or idea. The AI can be prompted to write in a specific tone or style suitable for short-form video.
ChatGPTJasper
Alternatives: ClaudeCopy.ai
Input: A video topic, target audience, and desired length (e.g., 30 seconds).
Output: A complete video script with dialogue and suggested visual cues.
A complete video script with dialogue and suggested visual cues.
Step 2
Text-to-Video Generation
Use a generative video model to create the base video clips from the script. Each scene or line of dialogue can be a separate prompt to generate a sequence of video clips.
SoraRunway
Alternatives: Kling AILuma AI
Input: The generated video script, broken down into scene-by-scene prompts.
Output: A collection of raw video clips corresponding to the script.
A collection of raw video clips corresponding to the script.
Step 3
Voiceover Generation
Use a text-to-speech tool to generate a high-quality, human-like voiceover for the video using the script. The voice can be cloned from an existing speaker or chosen from a library.
ElevenLabsPlay.ht
Alternatives: Resemble AI
Input: The final video script.
Output: An MP3 audio file of the voiceover.
An MP3 audio file of the voiceover.
Step 4
Video Editing & Assembly
Assemble the generated video clips and the voiceover in a video editor. Use an AI-powered editor to automatically add subtitles, background music, and transitions.
DescriptRunway
Input: The raw video clips and the voiceover audio file.
Output: A fully edited, final video file ready for publishing.
