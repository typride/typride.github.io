Academic Writing Workflow

Accelerate academic writing while maintaining rigor and originality. Uses AI to assist with literature review, evidence synthesis, outlining, and drafting, while keeping the human researcher in control of intellectual content. Can reduce literature review time by 50% and overall writing time by 30-40 hours per paper.

researchersstudentsanalysts

Workflow

Step 1
Literature discovery
Find the most relevant papers for your research question. Extract key findings and create a structured summary of the existing literature.
Elicit
Alternatives: Perplexity
Input: A research question or hypothesis
Output: Structured summary of relevant papers and key findings
Structured summary of relevant papers and key findings
Step 2
Evidence synthesis
Find the scientific consensus on key claims in your paper. Identify where there is strong agreement and where open research questions remain.
Consensus
Input: Key claims and questions from literature review
Output: Consensus view with supporting and conflicting evidence
Consensus view with supporting and conflicting evidence
Step 3
Deep source analysis
Upload key sources and ask detailed questions to identify connections between papers, find contradictions, and map how sources support your thesis.
NotebookLM
Alternatives: Claude
Input: Key source documents (PDFs, papers)
Output: Cross-source analysis with connections and contradictions
Cross-source analysis with connections and contradictions
Step 4
Drafting and revision
Draft sections of the paper with AI assistance, improve the clarity of arguments, and refine transitions between sections. Always review and revise AI-generated content critically.
ChatGPT
Alternatives: Claude
Input: Research synthesis and outline
Output: Complete paper draft
Complete paper draft
Step 5
Final editing and plagiarism check
Correct grammar, improve clarity, and run the plagiarism checker to ensure originality.
Grammarly
Input: Paper draft
Output: Publication-ready paper
