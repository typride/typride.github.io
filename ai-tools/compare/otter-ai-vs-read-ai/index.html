Pros & Cons

Otter.ai

Pros
  • Highly accurate real-time transcription for live meetings
  • Effective AI-powered summaries and action item extraction
  • Robust speaker identification and diarization
  • Seamless integration with popular video conferencing platforms
Cons
  • Transcription accuracy can be impacted by poor audio quality or strong accents
  • Free tier has significant limitations on transcription minutes
  • Less suited for long-form audio content editing compared to dedicated tools

Read.ai

Pros
  • Generous free plan with up to 5 meetings per month
  • Extremely detailed post-meeting reports with summaries, topics, and action items
  • Provides a personalized meeting coach with feedback on your speaking habits
  • Powerful enterprise search capabilities across all transcribed content
Cons
  • The AI assistant joining the call can feel intrusive to some participants
  • The sheer amount of data in the reports can be overwhelming at first

  • Collaborative note-taking during lectures or webinars

Read.ai

  • Automating note-taking for virtual meetings on Zoom, Teams, and Meet
  • Creating a searchable archive of all company meetings
  • Coaching teams on their meeting habits and communication styles
