Overview

Casetext CoCounsel

Casetext CoCounsel is an AI legal research assistant that helps law firms streamline their workflows. It uses advanced AI to help with tasks like legal research, document review, and drafting legal memos, saving lawyers valuable time and resources.

Elicit

An AI research assistant for academic literature review. Searches and summarizes research papers, extracts key findings, and helps identify relevant studies.

Pros & Cons

Casetext CoCounsel

Pros
  • Significantly speeds up legal research and document review.
  • Integrates with trusted legal databases for accurate results.
  • Can help reduce operational costs for law firms.
Cons
  • The cost can be a significant investment for smaller firms.
  • There is a learning curve to effectively use the AI prompts.
  • Some users have reported a decline in performance over time.

Elicit

Pros
  • Understands research methodology, not just keywords
  • Extracts structured data from papers (sample size, methods, findings)
  • Saves weeks of manual paper sifting for lit reviews
  • Good at surfacing relevant papers you wouldn't find via keyword search
  • Free tier is usable for small projects
Cons
  • Coverage gaps in some niche fields
  • Summaries can miss nuance in complex studies
  • Limited to academic papers (not patents, reports, etc.)
  • Plus plan needed for serious research volume

Use Cases

Casetext CoCounsel

  • Conducting legal research and finding relevant case law.
  • Reviewing and analyzing large volumes of legal documents.
  • Drafting legal memos, briefs, and other legal documents.

Elicit

  • Systematic literature review and paper discovery
  • Extracting key findings across multiple papers
  • Research question formulation and refinement
  • Identifying methodology patterns across studies
  • Building annotated bibliographies

Our Take

Elicit has a higher user rating (4.6 vs 4.5). Elicit offers a free tier, making it easier to try before you buy.
