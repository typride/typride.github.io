Overview

Casetext CoCounsel

Casetext CoCounsel is an AI legal research assistant that helps law firms streamline their workflows. It uses advanced AI to help with tasks like legal research, document review, and drafting legal memos, saving lawyers valuable time and resources.

Consensus

An AI-powered search engine for scientific research that finds and summarizes evidence from peer-reviewed papers. Designed to answer questions with evidence-based scientific consensus rather than opinion.

Pros & Cons

Casetext CoCounsel

Pros
  • Significantly speeds up legal research and document review.
  • Integrates with trusted legal databases for accurate results.
  • Can help reduce operational costs for law firms.
Cons
  • The cost can be a significant investment for smaller firms.
  • There is a learning curve to effectively use the AI prompts.
  • Some users have reported a decline in performance over time.

Consensus

Pros
  • Answers backed by peer-reviewed papers, not opinions
  • Consensus meter shows agreement level across studies
  • Great for settling debates with actual evidence
  • Simple question-in, evidence-out interface
  • Copilot feature synthesizes findings across papers
Cons
  • Less depth than Elicit for individual paper analysis
  • Coverage depends on available open-access papers
  • Consensus meter can oversimplify nuanced topics
  • Limited to scientific literature (not industry reports or grey literature)

Use Cases

Casetext CoCounsel

  • Conducting legal research and finding relevant case law.
  • Reviewing and analyzing large volumes of legal documents.
  • Drafting legal memos, briefs, and other legal documents.

Consensus

  • Answering questions with scientific evidence
  • Checking claims against peer-reviewed research
  • Understanding scientific consensus on a topic
  • Evidence-based decision making
  • Quick literature scans before deep dives

Our Take

Both tools are rated equally at 4.5/5. Consensus offers a free tier, making it easier to try before you buy.
