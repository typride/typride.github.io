Gemini

Google's flagship multimodal AI model, deeply integrated with Google Workspace (Docs, Sheets, Gmail). Gemini 2.5 Pro leads on many benchmarks and features a 1M token context window. Deep Research mode autonomously browses the web and produces structured reports.

Pros & Cons

Cohere

Pros
  • Strong focus on enterprise needs, including security and data privacy
  • Excellent RAG capabilities for grounding LLMs with proprietary data
  • Offers powerful embedding and reranking models for improved search relevance
  • Flexible deployment options, including on-premise and cloud providers
Cons
  • Pricing can be complex and less transparent for smaller users compared to simpler API providers
  • May require more technical expertise to integrate and optimize compared to out-of-the-box solutions
  • Less public community support compared to some open-source or more widely adopted LLMs

Gemini

Pros
  • Native multimodality across all input types
  • Seamless Google Workspace integration
  • Deep Research produces well-cited reports
  • 1M token context window on advanced models
Cons
  • Best features require Google One AI Premium subscription
  • Can occasionally produce inaccurate information

Use Cases

Cohere

  • Building RAG-powered chatbots and search engines
  • Semantic search and information retrieval
  • Text summarization and content generation for enterprise
  • Customizable LLMs for specific business domains

Gemini

  • Multimodal analysis (text, images, audio, video)
  • Deep research with autonomous web browsing
  • Integration with Google Docs, Sheets, and Gmail
  • Coding assistance via Gemini in IDEs
  • Summarizing long documents with audio overviews

Our Take

Gemini has a higher user rating (4.7 vs 4.2). Both tools offer a free tier, so you can try each before committing.
